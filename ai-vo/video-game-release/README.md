# The Chronicles of Aethelgard — AI VO Campaign

Concept ad campaign · Gemini TTS · Narrator VO · 4 spots

gemini-tts · ai-voiceover · ad-production · prompt-engineering · fantasy

## Overview

A concept advertising campaign for *The Chronicles of Aethelgard*, a fictional fantasy adventure game. The project demonstrates a full ad versioning workflow — from a hero :60s spot down through :30s, :15s, and :06s cutdowns — using AI-generated voiceover produced entirely with Google Gemini TTS.

Scripts were AI-assisted and refined to include detailed performance direction, guiding the voice model through emotional tone, pacing, and character transitions across each spot length.

## Deliverables

- **:60s** — hero spot, full narrative arc from world-building to CTA
- **:30s** — condensed with core hooks and GameStop pre-order CTA
- **:15s** — action-forward, platform-focused
- **:06s** — bumper, single message, hard CTA

## Tools & approach

- **TTS engine:** Google Gemini TTS
- **Voice:** Single narrator — warm, mentor-archetype character voice
- **Prompting:** Inline performance direction embedded in script (tone, energy, pacing cues per line)
- **Scripts:** AI-assisted, human-refined

## What to notice

Each cutdown isn't just a trim — the scripts were restructured to lead with the strongest hook for that format. The :06s strips everything down to brand name, release date, and retailer. The :60s opens with atmosphere and earns the CTA. This mirrors standard advertising versioning practice applied to an AI production workflow.
