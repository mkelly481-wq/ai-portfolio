# Aetheria — AI Ad Campaign

Concept ad campaign · Gemini TTS · Gemini image gen · VO · Print · Bottle label · Wellness / Satire

gemini-tts · gemini-image · ai-voiceover · ai-image-gen · ad-production · prompt-engineering · wellness · satire

## Overview

A fully AI-produced concept advertising campaign for *Aetheria*, a fictional ultra-premium wellness drink targeting the spiritual girlie demographic — think Coachella, sound baths, and crystals on the nightstand. The campaign plays wellness tropes completely straight, with zero winking, which is exactly what makes it work. Tagline: *Awaken Your Goddess Rising Energy.*

The creative concept originated from a real overheard phrase — "goddess rising energy" — and was developed into a full campaign through detailed creative direction: establishing character voice, comedic register, visual world, and brand identity before any AI generation began. Scripts were human-conceived and creatively directed, with AI assistance for refinement and production.

## Deliverables

**Voiceover spots — Gemini TTS**

- **:30s — The Awakening** — hero spot; breathy ethereal narrator, crystal singing bowl SFX, full goddess rising narrative arc
- **:15s — The Activation** — social cutdown; third eye focus, punchy stacked copy, "Moon-Charged. Frequency-Aligned. Goddess-Approved."
- **:06s — The Frequency** — bumper; single crystalline moment, "Drink the frequency of your highest self."

**Print advertisement — Gemini image gen (Google AI Studio)**

- **Magazine vertical full-page** — woman on a misty mountaintop, arms outstretched, holding a glowing violet bottle, bathed in golden god rays and lens flare; title and tagline in gold

**Bottle label — Gemini image gen (Google AI Studio)**

- **Flat label design** — deep violet and gold; goddess figure rising from a lotus illustration; ornate decorative borders; all brand copy and product details integrated into the label layout

## Tools & approach

- **TTS engine:** Google Gemini TTS
- **Image gen:** Google Gemini (via Google AI Studio)
- **Voice:** Breathy, ethereal narrator — guided meditation meets wellness influencer, 0.80x speed, slightly raised pitch
- **Prompting:** Detailed character briefs established tone, class persona, and comedic register before generation; negative prompting used to isolate label design from scene-based outputs; SFX and pacing direction embedded throughout scripts
- **Scripts:** Human-conceived and creatively directed; AI-assisted for refinement

## What to notice

The entire campaign holds a single comedic register — sincere, aspirational, and completely committed — across five distinct assets. The VO scripts escalate perfectly across spot lengths: the :30s builds the world, the :15s distills it to a flex, the :06s reduces it to one crystalline image. The print ad and bottle label share the same visual language (violet, gold, goddess, god rays) so the campaign feels like a real brand system, not a collection of experiments. The bottle label in particular required negative prompting to redirect the model away from scene generation — a practical prompt engineering technique worth noting.
