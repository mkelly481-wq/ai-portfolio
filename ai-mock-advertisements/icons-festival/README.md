# ICONS Festival — AI Ad Campaign

gemini-tts · gemini-image · ai-voiceover · ai-image-gen · event-marketing · prompt-engineering · pop-culture · social-media

## Overview

A fully AI-produced concept advertising campaign for *ICONS Festival*, a fictional three-day pop music festival held in Las Vegas over Memorial Day Weekend, May 23–25. Lineup: MYNDI, Lady Sparkles, Glitter Riot, Jennifer Sampson, and Puss n Boots. Tagline: *Come Iconic. Leave Legendary.*

The campaign targets the gays and gals — loud, fashion-forward, pop-obsessed — and is built to match that energy at every touchpoint. VO, posters, and social assets all share the same maximalist, high-glamour visual and tonal identity. The creative brief was simple: smack the audience in the face with it.

## Deliverables

**Voiceover spots — Gemini TTS**

- **:30s — The Announcement** — hero spot; glamorous British party girl narrator, massive orchestral hit opening, full lineup and CTA delivered at full diva energy
- **:15s — The Tease** — social cutdown; rapid-fire lineup drop, punchy and immediate
- **:06s — The Drop** — bumper; single explosive moment, "This is your moment, babes."

**Festival posters — Gemini image gen (Google AI Studio)**

- **Maximalist glamour poster** — glitter ICONS title in pink-rimmed gold, aerial Las Vegas Strip at night, fireworks, confetti cannons, diamonds; full lineup and dates below
- **Pop art poster** — hot pink, gold, and yellow; bold comic-style starburst, halftone textures, each artist name in its own distinct graphic treatment; crowd silhouette at base

**Social media lineup graphic — Gemini image gen (Google AI Studio)**

- **Square Instagram graphic** — pop art style, yellow and hot pink, each artist name in its own explosive banner treatment; "THE LINEUP IS HERE" header; event details footer

## Tools & approach

- **TTS engine:** Google Gemini TTS
- **Image gen:** Google Gemini (via Google AI Studio)
- **Voice:** Glamorous British party girl, early 20s — theatrical and explosive, not an announcer but a fan who got handed a microphone
- **Prompting:** Detailed character and scene briefs established energy and persona before generation; phonetic spelling used for artist name MYNDI to achieve correct TTS pronunciation; two distinct visual styles prompted and compared for poster format; output reviewed and corrected after model hallucinated a sixth artist in an early iteration
- **Scripts:** Human-conceived and creatively directed; AI-assisted for refinement

## What to notice

This campaign demonstrates range across format and energy — it's a completely different creative register from the other campaigns in this portfolio. The VO character required specific scene and persona briefs to nail the right tone: not a polished announcer, but an excited fan with a mic. Phonetic spelling of "MYNDI" as "MEN-DI" in the TTS input was a practical fix to achieve correct pronunciation — a small but real prompt engineering detail. The two poster styles show how the same brief can be interpreted across different visual languages, and the lineup graphic demonstrates social-first format thinking. Output QA also caught a hallucinated artist in an early poster iteration, which was corrected before final asset selection.
