# The Chronicles of Aethelgard — AI Ad Campaign

Concept ad campaign · Gemini TTS · Gemini image gen · VO · Box art · Print

gemini-tts · gemini-image · ai-voiceover · ai-image-gen · ad-production · prompt-engineering · fantasy

## Overview

A fully AI-produced concept advertising campaign for *The Chronicles of Aethelgard*, a fictional fantasy adventure game. The project spans multiple formats and media — voiceover spots, box art concepts, and a print advertisement — all produced using Google's Gemini AI tools and built to demonstrate end-to-end AI creative production across a single campaign.

The campaign follows a young warrior and his wizard mentor on an epic quest — a narrative thread that runs consistently across every asset, from the tone of the VO scripts to the visual composition of the print ad.

## Deliverables

**Voiceover spots — Gemini TTS**

- **:60s** — hero spot, full narrative arc from world-building to CTA
- **:30s** — condensed with core hooks and GameStop pre-order CTA
- **:15s** — action-forward, platform-focused
- **:06s** — bumper, single message, hard CTA

**Box art concepts — Gemini image gen (Google AI Studio)**

- **Concept 1** — hero and wizard on a stone bridge, surveying the kingdom ahead; dark void creature looming above
- **Concept 2** — the Aether-Bound Blade in a stone, ancient face emerging from storm clouds; painterly landscape style
- **Concept 3** — triptych panel layout featuring the wizard, warrior, and Storm-Giant; dark comic illustration style

**Print advertisement — Gemini image gen (Google AI Studio)**

- **Magazine full-page (vertical)** — warrior and wizard in action foreground, castle and dark creature behind; title, tagline, and platform CTA integrated into the layout

## Tools & approach

- **TTS engine:** Google Gemini TTS
- **Image gen:** Google Gemini (via Google AI Studio)
- **Voice:** Single narrator — warm, mentor-archetype character voice
- **Prompting:** Inline performance direction embedded in VO scripts; detailed visual and compositional direction for image assets
- **Scripts:** AI-assisted, human-refined

## What to notice

This campaign was built to feel like a real production — not a collection of disconnected experiments. The VO scripts include per-line performance direction guiding tone, pacing, and energy across four spot lengths. The box art concepts explore three distinct visual styles within the same world. The print ad follows standard magazine ad composition with title, tagline, characters, threat, and CTA all working together. Everything stays in the Google AI ecosystem, making this a demonstration of what a cohesive Gemini-powered creative workflow looks like in practice.
